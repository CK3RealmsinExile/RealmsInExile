### Historic Dynasties ###
#
# Âdro Clan		(Currently) First Kings of Nurn, rose among the clans during the Watchful Peace, before Mordor reemerged.
# 				They were crushed by the Variags during the reign of Ôvatha VI, who proceeded to conquer Núrn, Chelkar and Lurmsakûn.
#				Reigned T.A.1767 - T.A.1977 (210 years)
#
# Caiarki Clan	Ôvatha VI installed his second son, Cairo I (lineofskakali3), on the throne.
#				Their reign continued after the fall of the Khandish Dominion. Within a few generations, many Nurniag traditions were adopted by the nobility. By this point, Mordor was rising once more and they became their puppet state. 
#				Conflict arose when the young King Yursal III (linenikalad13), attempted to rise against Mordor because he viewed his status as a puppet ruler to be humiliating. The would-be rebellion was swiftly put down by the Nazgul and his close family summarily executed.
#				Reigned T.A.1987 - T.A.2259 (272 years)
#
# Îbo Clan		Mordor installed Sakun V (lineibo21) a descendant of the Âdro kings, as a puppet ruler and Núrn lost many of its privileges as Mordors power increased. Despite its length, their reign was mostly unremarkable with few internal conflicts
#				Over time, they grew ever more decadent and weak to the point they were utterly inefficient in their administration. This culminated in a Mordor-backed coup after the death of Ôthour III (lineibo43) to prevent his son Akhev (lineiboweakling) from inheriting the throne.
# 				Reigned T.A.1977 - T.A.2648 (671 years)
#
# Ârki Clan		In a coup backed by Mordor, the Castellan of Thaurband and de-facto ruler of Núrn, Yursal IV (lineudo11) seized the throne.
#				His clan of Mordor loyalists has been ruling Núrn ever since. The War of the Ring occured during the reign of King Ôthour VI (lineudo35) and his regime collapsed when the slaves of Núrn were freed in the aftermath of the Fall of Barad-Dûr.
#				Reigned T.A.2648 - T.A.3020 (372 years)
#
#
### Cultures of Nurn ###
# The Nurniags - A culture of easterling slavers, very conservative Sauron worshippers
# The Nurnoth - Daen descended slaves of Nurn, no rulers during the start date
