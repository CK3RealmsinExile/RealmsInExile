# House of Dulguzagar, marshalls of the Thôn an-Khârlôkh - The Army of the Southern Dragon #Dulgu the Black, 1367, renegade son of Dâirukhôr of Dûshera
# Line of Azrubên of Rómenna, Numenorean governer of An-Balkûmagân, forefather of the kings of the same region
# Other

# Internal History: An-Balkûmagân established its own kinship and line of tradition after the downfall of Numenor.
# The returned Storm-King in the 1600s brought An-Balkûmagân and its kings back under his sway during the years of the Thôn an-Khârlôkh, but in the end
# they returned south, not willing to expend their energy and men for a war that had only resulted in the loss of their king. Several hundred years later, the 
# Akhôrahil decsended in the night upon Zagar-an-Adun, into the heartland of An-Balkûmagân, and demanded the submission of the Anbalukkhôri kings. The reigning king, struck with terror, accepted.
# Since then the kings in An-Balkûmagân have retained their place, but they are subordinated to the Storm-Marshall of the house of Dulgu of the reborn Thôn an-Khârlôkh.
# They have served faithfully since then, unwilling to risk extermination at the hands of the eternal Storm-King's wrath.
