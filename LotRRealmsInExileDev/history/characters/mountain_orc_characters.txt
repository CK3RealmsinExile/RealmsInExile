# 
# 1267 T.A. A  Balcog emerges from either Ered Mithrin or forodwaith. Wishing to restore the cult of Ulûkai he'd amassed a great number of orcish warriors (and supported by the Nazgûl-lord on his quest to found the realm of Angmar) he attacks Mount Gundabad in force during the summer but is unable to take it immediately

# 1268-1974 T.A. Mount Gundabad serves as the principal orcish citadel in the Hithaeglir. Gundabad’s strength is used extensively by the Witchking in his wars against the successor realms of Arnor. At first this is done covertly, but beginning in the mid-19th century, also more openly.
# T.A. 1975 T.A. Angmar falls, Gundabad is no longer a pupet under Angmar, and gain some of it's forces that belonged to the eastern Angmar borders. The line of Trupalog-kai can not hold the kingdom of gundabad togather and it falls apart into duchies.

# 2055 T.A. Azog the defiler conquers Gundabad from Trupalog-kai orcs, and restores the Kingdom of Gundabad whom de jure land both have the Worauthai Madur & Uruk Ongmair cultured orcs in it, he unifies them into the Ghrazim, the Rack-Horde (in orcish), Gundabad Orcs
# 2793-2799 T.A. War of the Dwarves and Orcs, end's with the death of Azog
# 2799 T.A  Bolg takes over up to the point of 2941 T.A. when the Battle of the five armies 2941, where he perishes 
# 2941 T.A Gorgol first son of Bolg, flees back to Gundabad and assumes control, with his forces weakened and with the shock of the loss of his father he fears he can not complete the Ulûkai quest, so dosn't immidetly assume the Ashdurbúk quest, which his Brother Gorgar view as weakness and plans to usurp him
